## Getting Help

### Before Asking for Help

1. Check the **README files** in the relevant repository
2. Search through **[existing issues](https://github.com/search?q=org%3Amortiscope&type=issues)** to see if your question has been answered
3. Review **[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/mortiscope/discussions)** for community Q&A
4. Consult the **[Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)** for community guidelines

### Ways to Get Support

#### 1. GitHub Discussions (Recommended)

For general questions, ideas, and conversations:

- [Browse existing discussions](https://github.com/orgs/mortiscope/discussions)
- Start a new discussion if your topic isn't covered
- Share your experiences and help others

#### 2. GitHub Issues

For bugs and feature requests:

- Use our [Bug Report Template](ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug_report.yml) for bugs
- Use our [Feature Request Template](ISSUE_TEMPLATE/feature_request.yml) for suggestions
- Provide detailed information to help us assist you

#### 3. Security Issues

For security vulnerabilities:

- **DO NOT** create public issues
- Follow our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) for responsible disclosure
- Contact maintainers privately

## Academic Inquiries

For research collaboration, thesis inquiries, or academic questions:

- Contact the maintainers directly through GitHub
- Include details about your research interests
- Mention if you're working in forensic science or artificial intelligence

## Community Guidelines

When seeking support:

- Be respectful and patient
- Provide context and detailed information
- Follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Help others when you can
- Update discussions/issues with solutions you discover

## Project Status

Mortiscope is a **thesis project** by two researchers. Please note that:

- Responses may take a few days
- Direct code contributions are currently closed
- We appreciate your understanding and patience
